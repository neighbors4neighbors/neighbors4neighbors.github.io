Connecting Those in Need

Working together with our non-profit partners and generous community partners and through the power of media, both on air and online, we are able to connect resources to those in our community that need it the most.

Our Mission

Neighbors 4 Neighbors is a nationally recognized nonprofit, created in the aftermath of Hurricane Andrew in 1992. The simple mission is to connect those in need with those who can help. Helping the Community

Neighbors 4 Neighbors is a 501(c)(3) non-profit organization that has connected those in need with those who can help in South Florida for the past 20 years.
